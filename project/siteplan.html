Site Plan: True Focus Photography

Site Name

True Focus Photography — This name reflects the goal of capturing sincere, emotionally focused moments in weddings, engagements, and family portraits.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a professional portfolio showcasing the photographer's work in weddings, engagements, and family portraits. Visitors can view example galleries, learn about the services offered, and contact the photographer through a form.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a session with the photographer?
  • Where can I see wedding or engagement photo samples?
  • What locations or areas does the photographer serve?

Color Scheme

  • #4A0D29 – Dark Burgundy (used for headers, nav bar, and footers)
  • #F3E8E2 – Warm Beige (used for page background)
  • #E4B7B2 – Blush Pink (used for buttons and accents)
  • #2C2C2C – Charcoal Gray (used for body text)
  • #FFFFFF – White (used for text on dark backgrounds)

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Headings
  • Lato – Body text
