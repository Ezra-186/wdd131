About True Focus Photography

At True Focus Photography, we believe every moment tells a story. From intimate weddings to family portraits, our passion is capturing the genuine emotion behind each frame.

Founded in 2010 by award-winning photographer Ezra Rivera, our team combines technical expertise with a creative eye. We specialize in:

  • Weddings & Engagements
  • Family & Portrait Sessions
  • Event Coverage

Our mission is simple: to provide a relaxed, enjoyable experience that leaves you with stunning images you'll cherish forever.
